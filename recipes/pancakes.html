Pancake Recipe

Description

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional for sweeter pancakes)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk (or 1 cup milk mixed with 1 tablespoon white vinegar or lemon juice)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (or vegetable oil)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Steps:

  1. Place a griddle or non-stick frying pan on your stovetop and set it to medium-high heat. Allow it to heat while you prepare the pancake batter.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, sugar (if using), baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the buttermilk (or milk and vinegar/lemon juice mixture), egg, melted butter (or oil), and vanilla extract (if using).
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir gently with a spoon or whisk until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps in the batter are okay.
  5. Lightly grease the hot griddle or pan with a small amount of butter or oil to prevent sticking.
  6. Ladle about 1/4 cup of pancake batter onto the hot griddle for each pancake. Use the back of the ladle to spread the batter into a circular shape.
  7. Cook the pancakes until you see small bubbles forming on the surface, and the edges start to look set, usually about 2-3 minutes.
  8. Carefully flip each pancake with a spatula and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until the second side is golden brown.
  9. Remove the pancakes from the griddle or pan and place them on a plate. Keep them warm in a low oven (around 200°F or 93°C) if you're making a large batch.
  10. Serve your pancakes while they're still warm. Top with butter, syrup, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or any other favorite toppings you prefer.
  11. Repeat the cooking process with the remaining batter until you've made all the pancakes.

Enjoy Your Pancakes!!!
